Project Overview

About Orange Grove Residences

Orange Grove Residences is a premium residential project spanning over 25 acres of lush greenery, offering a perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience. Designed by award-winning architects, this masterpiece features spacious apartments, villas, and penthouses with world-class amenities.

Located in the most sought-after neighborhood, Orange Grove Residences provides easy access to major business districts, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and entertainment hubs. The project is built with sustainable materials and incorporates eco-friendly practices, making it an ideal choice for environmentally conscious homebuyers.
